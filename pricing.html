Questions & Answers

You might have questions about pricing, we have answers for you.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes. You can use your own domain (e.g. mystore.com) with your Webx website. We also provide a free webx.pk domain name (e.g. mystore.webx.pk) to all stores at the time of sign up.

Is a custom domain included in my package?

No. Custom domains are not included in packages. If you already own a domain or buy a new one, it can easily be pointed to your website.

Do I need a separate web hosting?

No. All Webx websites are hosted on our secure and fast servers. There is no need to buy separate web hosting service.

What are your bandwidth fees?

There is no additional bandwidth fee. All Webx packages include unlimited bandwidth.

Is my data safe and secure?

The security and uptime of your website is our top priority. We protect your site and data with the industry standard security and encryption, and we have a 99.99% average uptime.

How can I increase my package products limit?

If at any point of time, you go out of product quota, you can increase it by either upgrading your package or releasing quota by deleting any existing inactive products.

Is there a setup fee?

No. There are no setup fees on any of our plans.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. You can sign up and use Webx for 14 days without making any payment. At the end of your trial, or when you decide to launch your store, you will need to pick a plan and make payment to activate it on your domain.

What is the minimum term of contract?

All Webx plans are month to month. Its up to you whether you sign up for a monthly, 6 monthly or an annual subscription.

What are your support fees?

All Webx packages include free dedicated support.

Are there any hidden charges?

There are no hidden charges. The price you see in the pricing table is what you pay.

Can I change my package later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your package at any time.

Can I cancel my account anytime?

Yes. If for any reason, you don’t want to continue your website with Webx, you can cancel your account anytime.
